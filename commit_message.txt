refactor(engine): split sync logic in acceptApproval to standalone helpers

Extracts the monolithic inner loop body of `acceptApproval` within
`packages/engine/src/vault.ts` into two discrete, atomic helpers:
- `syncStagedEntry` handles file resolution, validation, parsing, and deletion.
- `syncOutputAssets` handles moving asset directories for output pages.

This successfully addresses the massive size of the approval loop
without breaking any of the heavy implicit dependencies within the engine,
making the codebase substantially easier to trace and reason about.

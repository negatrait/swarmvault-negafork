docs: Add PORTING_ANALYSIS.md evaluating WASM/Go/Rust ports

Added an architectural analysis document evaluating the feasibility and
benefits of porting SwarmVault to WebAssembly, Go, or Rust.

This analysis addresses the goals of shrinking the memory footprint,
compiling to a standalone CLI binary without dependencies, and keeping
WASM as a compatible target. The document identifies Rust as the ideal
candidate to meet all criteria and outlines a recommended roadmap.
